What to watch

Drink attach. Fri 10 Jul night mix ran food 68.1% / alcohol 28.2% / non-alc 2.5% against the regular benchmark of 61 / 35 / 3.5 — booze under-indexing by ~7 points on a night EatClub was 15% of takings. This is the one metric trending the wrong way, and it compounds HG's existing bar-spend decline (−30% YoY per open day).
Zero returning customers. All 28 redeemed tables to date are NEW. Until someone comes back at full price, this channel is discounting, not acquiring — that number is the whole business case.
Table-level attach is not measurable. Insights order-level explores are locked behind "Upgrade to Custom" on the account that can see HG, so EatClub bills can't be matched to POS orders. All mix figures above are NIGHT level (every table, not just EatClub).
